Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

1. The 14-Day Free Trial

We provide a comprehensive 14-day free trial for all new workspaces. You are not charged during this period, giving you full access to evaluate PolyVision's GPS tracking, photo logs, voice calls, and other features before committing to a paid plan.

2. Subscription Cancellation

You may cancel your PolyVision subscription at any time from your administrator dashboard. Your workspace will remain active until the end of your current paid billing cycle. We do not provide refunds or credits for any partial billing periods.

3. Refund Eligibility

Because we offer a 14-day free trial to thoroughly evaluate the platform, all standard subscription payments (₹149/mo, ₹699/6mo, ₹999/yr) processed via Razorpay are strictly non-refundable once the billing cycle begins.

4. Enterprise One-Time Plans

The ₹25,000 Enterprise one-time license is non-refundable once the white-label environment has been provisioned and deployed, as this involves dedicated engineering setup and resources.

5. Exceptions & Terminations

No refunds or credits will be issued under any circumstances if your workspace is suspended or terminated for violating our Terms of Service or Acceptable Use policy.

6. Contact & Support

If you have billing questions or require assistance, please email support@polyvision.shiot.in or WhatsApp +91 73859 51732. We aim to respond within 24-48 business hours.
