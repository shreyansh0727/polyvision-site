Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

1. Subscription Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time. Your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing period. No refunds are issued for partial billing periods.

2. Refund Eligibility

PolyVision offers a 7-day money-back guarantee on your first subscription payment. If you're not satisfied within the first 7 days, contact us and we'll issue a full refund — no questions asked.

Refunds do not apply to renewal payments or subsequent subscriptions. Only the first-ever payment for a new workspace qualifies.

3. One-Time Plans (Enterprise)

Enterprise one-time payments are non-refundable after 30 days. Within the first 30 days, a full refund is available upon request if the service fails to meet its documented specifications.

4. How to Request a Refund

Email support@shiot.in or WhatsApp +91 73859 51732 with your registered email and workspace name. We process refunds within 5-7 business days.

5. Exceptions

Refunds are not provided if: (a) the subscription was purchased using a promotional discount, (b) the account violated our Terms of Service, or (c) the refund request is made after the eligible period.
