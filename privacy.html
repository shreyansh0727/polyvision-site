Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2025

We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data PolyVision collects, why we collect it, and what we do with it.

1. What we collect

You give us: Email, company name, and team member details when you create a workspace.

We collect automatically: GPS location during active tracking, device model, app version, and FCM token for notifications.

2. Why we collect it

  • To show employee locations on the admin map
  • To trigger geofence alerts when employees enter or leave zones
  • To process task assignments and status updates
  • To enable voice calling between admin and field staff
  • To generate attendance reports
  • To send push notifications

3. We don't sell your data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information. Period. The only people who see employee location data are the admins in the same workspace.

4. How we protect it

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • Firebase Authentication on every API call
  • Google Cloud SQL with encryption at rest
  • Role-based access: employees see only their own data

5. Data retention

Raw GPS points are kept for 90 days, then automatically deleted. Account data is kept while your workspace is active. You can request deletion anytime at privacy@polyvision.shiot.in.

6. Your rights

You can access, correct, export, or delete your data. Just email us. If an employee withdraws consent for tracking, their location stops being collected immediately.

7. Contact

Questions? privacy@polyvision.shiot.in
