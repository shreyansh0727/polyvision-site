What's inside

The tools you actually need

We didn't stuff it with features nobody asked for. Every tool here came from talking to business owners running field teams across India.

Live Map

See everyone on one map. Who's online, who's idle, who's in the right neighborhood. Updates every few seconds — no refresh needed.

Geofence Zones

Draw boundaries on the map. Get an alert when someone enters or leaves a zone. Perfect for marking client sites and no-go areas.

Tasks & Assignments

Send tasks to employees with location, priority, and deadline. They accept, work on it, and mark it done. You see it all in real time.

One-tap Calling

Voice call any employee right from the map. No phone numbers needed. Works on any internet connection — WiFi or mobile data.

Clock-in / Clock-out

Employees mark attendance with GPS proof. Breaks, overtime, daily summaries — all logged. Export anytime for payroll.

Route Playback

Replay anyone's movement over any date range. See exactly where they went, when, and for how long. Great for dispute resolution.

Quick setup

Four steps and you're live

No IT team. No server setup. Install, register, invite, track. Takes about 5 minutes.

Step 01

Install the app

Download from our website or Play Store. Works on any Android phone running Android 7+.

Step 02

Create workspace

Sign up with your email, name your company. You get an invite code to share with your team.

Step 03

Invite employees

Share the code. Each employee signs up, enters the code, and they're connected to your workspace.

Step 04

Start tracking

That's it. Employees appear on your live map. Set up geofences, assign tasks, and you're running.

Trust

Your data stays yours

We take security seriously because we're handling people's location. Here's what protects your data.

Firebase Auth

Every login is verified by Google's authentication system. No weak passwords, no fake accounts.

Encrypted everywhere

All location data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Your employees' whereabouts are never exposed.

Role-based access

Employees see only their own data. Admins see their team. Nobody sees what they shouldn't.

Google Cloud

Hosted on Google's infrastructure. Automatic backups, 99.9% uptime, enterprise-grade physical security.
